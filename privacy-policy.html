Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 19, 2025 | Last Updated: September 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Honkit! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Key Points:

  • We only collect information necessary to provide and improve Honkit
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • You have control over your data and privacy settings
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Name and email address (required for registration)
  • Username and profile information
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Bio/description (optional)

Content You Create:

  • Decision images ("honks") you upload
  • Decision titles and options you create
  • Proof images you share
  • Votes and interactions with other users' content

Communications:

  • Messages you send to our support team
  • Feedback and survey responses
  • User reports and safety concerns

Information We Collect Automatically

Usage Information:

  • How you interact with the App (features used, time spent)
  • Decisions you view, vote on, or create
  • Follow relationships and social interactions
  • App performance and error data

Device Information:

  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • Mobile carrier and network information
  • Device identifiers (for security and fraud prevention)
  • App version and settings

Location Information:

  • General location (city/region) for content personalization
  • We do NOT collect precise GPS location
  • Location data is optional and can be disabled

3. How We Use Your Information

To Provide Our Service

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and content to other users
  • Enable decision creation, voting, and social features
  • Process premium subscriptions and payments
  • Send important service notifications

To Improve and Personalize

  • Recommend relevant decisions and users to follow
  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Customize your feed and experience
  • Provide customer support

For Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and community guidelines
  • Investigate reports and safety concerns
  • Protect against technical issues and security threats

For Legal and Business Purposes

  • Comply with legal obligations and requests
  • Respond to court orders and law enforcement
  • Protect our rights and the rights of our users
  • Business operations and analytics (in aggregated, anonymized form)

4. How We Share Your Information

Information We Share Publicly

  • Your profile information (name, username, bio, profile picture)
  • Decisions ("honks") you create and make public
  • Your votes on other users' decisions (visible to decision creators)
  • Your followers and following lists (unless you make your profile private)

Information We Share with Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party providers who help us operate Honkit:

  • Cloud hosting and storage services (Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics services (for app performance and usage insights)
  • Customer support tools
  • Payment processors (for premium subscriptions)
  • Security and fraud prevention services

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

  • In response to court orders or legal process
  • To comply with law enforcement requests
  • To protect our rights or the safety of our users
  • To prevent illegal activities or violations of our Terms

Business Transfers

If Honkit is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Account Controls

  • Profile Privacy: Make your profile private or public
  • Content Control: Delete your decisions and content
  • Following: Control who can follow you and see your content
  • Notifications: Customize what notifications you receive

Data Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of certain data processing activities

Marketing Communications

  • You can opt out of promotional emails at any time
  • Service-related notifications cannot be disabled
  • You can control push notifications through your device settings

6. Data Security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures

Your Responsibility

  • Keep your account credentials secure
  • Don't share your password with others
  • Report suspicious account activity immediately
  • Be mindful of what you share publicly

7. International Data Transfers

Honkit operates globally, and your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Using service providers that comply with international data protection standards
  • Implementing appropriate contractual protections
  • Following applicable legal frameworks for data transfers

8. Children's Privacy

Honkit is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

For users between 13-18, we encourage parental involvement and provide additional privacy protections.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and for legitimate business purposes:

  • Active accounts: We retain your information while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Most information is deleted within 30 days
  • Legal requirements: Some information may be retained longer for legal compliance
  • Safety and security: Certain data may be retained to prevent fraud and abuse

10. Regional Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

European Users (GDPR)

European users have rights including:

  • Access, rectification, and erasure of personal data
  • Data portability and restriction of processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll notify you through the App or by email
  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date
  • Material changes will be clearly highlighted
  • Continued use of Honkit constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your privacy rights:

General Privacy Questions:

  • Email: support@honkit.app
  • Support: support@honkit.app

Data Protection Officer:

  • Email: support@honkit.app

Privacy Rights Requests:

To exercise your privacy rights (access, deletion, correction), please contact us at privacy@honkit.app with "Privacy Rights Request" in the subject line.

13. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of September 19, 2025, and was last updated on September 19, 2025.

Your privacy matters to us. If you have any concerns or questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
